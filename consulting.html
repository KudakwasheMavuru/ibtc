Consulting

IBTC Founder, Perpetue Mukamusinga , is passionate about helping others succeed in the coffee business.

We are here to help you get there!

Perpetue Mukamusinga - "Queen P" is dedicated to working with people at every stage of the coffee process, from farmers to roasters and coffee shop owners. She doesn’t just have certifications and knowledge of coffee, he actually works in every aspect of the coffee industry.

From roasting coffee, and the wholesale and distribution of fine coffee and teas to designing, building, and managing their cafe, Queen P has a deep understanding and connection to what it takes to have a successful coffee business. Whether it is traveling to your location to train you and your team or consulting you on your business plan or coffee shop design, hiring the team at IBTC is a business decision you won’t regret.
